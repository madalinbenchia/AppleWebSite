MacBook Air

Description

The new MacBook Air is thin, light and durable enough to take it wherever you go - and strong enough to do anything when you got there.


Flash memory

Flash storage in MacBook Air is up to 17x faster than a 5400-rpm notebook hard drive. So everything you do is snappy and responsive. MacBook Air even wakes up fast, thanks to flash storage and fifth-generation Intel Core processors.



Battery long haul. Instant ignition. Even after a month break.

Because flash storage saves space and leaves room for a bigger battery, you have more time for the things you care about; such as browsing the web, editing photos and watching videos. Get up to 12 hours of battery life on a single charge for 13-inch model. Send the MacBook Air to sleep and go into standby mode to conserve battery life.


720p HD FaceTime camera.

Friends and family will see you better HD camera FaceTime 720p. Widescreen format allows everyone to fit into the framework without crowding around the screen.
