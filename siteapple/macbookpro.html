MacBook Pro 15 Retina

Description

MacBook Pro has retina display, flash storage and quad-core processor in a thin and light model. Measuring only 1.8 cm thick and weighing only 2.02 kg, this completely redesigned MacBook Pro sets a new standard in terms of performance and portability for professional users.



Retina display

The 15-inch model has over 5 million pixels. So you can retouch your photos or edit a home movie in HD and experience an astounding level of clarity.Text is razor sharp, too,so even everyday things like browsing the web and revising a document are better than ever.



Intel Core I7

Retina display MacBook Pro includes the latest Intel Core i7 processors with four cores at 2.5 GHz frequencies with Turbo Boost speeds up to 3.7 GHz, NVIDIA GeForce GT 750M dedicated graphics, 16 GB of fast memory to 1600 MHz and flash storage.
