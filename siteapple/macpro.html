Mac Pro

For a big step forward , sometimes you need to change direction .

Description

Designed to blend with dual GPU graphics station , PCIe flash storage , Thunderbolt2 high-performance Xeon processors last generation ultrafast memory and support for 4K video format , the new Mac Pro offers top performance from all points of view .


New Ideas

When we started working on the new Mac Pro , we considered each element that defines a professional computer - graphics , storage, expansion , processing power and memory. We set the objective of designing each of these elements in the most visionary and most advanced way . And when we finished , the result was something completely new .



Processor

The new Mac Pro is pure force , starting with Intel Xeon processor E5 new generation , available with 4, 6 , 8 or 12 cores on a single pill . And , with an L3 cache of up to 30 MB , a band of 40 GB / s PCI Express Gen 3 and the floating -point instructions 256 bits, never shalt not lack of speed .


One innovative fan

To cool a device so powerful , it took an incredibly innovative ventilation system . Instead of adding additional fans , we designed a unique fan , biggest , which draw air upward through the inlet at the bottom . As the air passes vertically through the center of the device , absorbs heat and leads to the top. It is simple and elegant - and amazingly quiet . To achieve this , we had to consider every detail : the number of blades , size, and shape up . Minimizing air resistance through the system , we were able to design a rotor blade fan with curved back , which revolves with fewer revolutions per minute , more effectively draw air through the rotation and creates much less noise.
